Build & share

Build your own film, then share it

A film is a look you build yourself — set each layer's colour, dye, grain, and contrast. Every film folds into a small code: share it as a link or QR, and anyone with Oqra can scan it and shoot your film.

Help

Support & questions

The fastest way to reach us is email — we read every message.

Email support

Found a bug or have a request? Email support@oqra.app with your iPhone model, iOS version, and a screenshot if you can.

Which iPhones does it run on?

iPhone 13 and newer, with iOS 18 or later.

Is it free?

It's free while we're in the TestFlight beta. It'll be on the App Store soon.

Do my photos stay private?

Yes — everything develops on your phone. No account, no servers, nothing uploaded. See the privacy policy.

Do I keep my original photo?

You can keep the untouched original — RAW included — saved next to the developed shot. Just turn on Keep RAW File in the app.
