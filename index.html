Orchestra of Aura

A hybrid camera that develops in real physics.

Oqra captures on iPhone, then develops every frame through a custom Metal engine that models real photographic physics — spectral exposure, characteristic curves, grain, halation, and dye. You don't pick a look; you build it.

Build & share

Build your own film, then share it

A film is a recipe you author, not a preset you scroll — tune each layer's spectral response, dye, grain, and contrast curve. Every film folds into a small code: share it as a link or QR, and anyone with Oqra can scan it and shoot your film.

Open a shared recipe

Oqra film stocks travel as a scannable code or an oqra.app/p/… link. Paste one here and download the recipe as a file — decoded entirely in your browser, nothing uploaded. Import the .fbp in Oqra and the look is reproduced by the film engine on your device.
