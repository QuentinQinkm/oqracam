iPhone · Film camera

A film camera built on
chemistry, not filters.

Oqra is a physics-based film camera for iPhone. You don't scroll a folder of LUTs — you author your own film: design emulsion layers, tune dye chemistry, and develop virtual film through a real spectral engine.

What makes it different

Real film engine

Spectral exposure, Hurter–Driffield response, dye absorption, grain, halation, and orange mask — simulated, not faked with a tone curve.

Author, don't pick

Every look is a recipe fed to the engine. Tune sensitizer wavelength, dye purity, contrast and base fog — there's no fixed filter list.

RAW & Live Photos

Capture in RAW / ProRAW and shoot Live Photos, then develop them through the same film pipeline.

Built into the hardware

Shoot from the iPhone Camera Control button and straight from the lock screen — Oqra lives where your camera already does.

Everything on device

No account, no servers, no analytics or tracking. Your photos, videos, and recipes never leave your phone.

Share your recipes

Hand a film stock you authored to a friend as a single scannable code — no export, no cloud.
