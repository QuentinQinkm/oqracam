# LumBo Cam — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: May 1, 2026_

LumBo Cam is a film-emulation photo and video app. This policy explains what information the app collects and how it is used.

## What we collect

**Nothing is sent off your device.** LumBo Cam does not require an account, does not transmit your photos, videos, or film recipes to any server, and does not include third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

The app requests the following system permissions, used only for the features you choose to enable:

- **Camera** — to capture photos and video inside LumBo Cam, including via the iPhone 16 Camera Control button and the lock-screen Locked Camera Capture extension.
- **Microphone** — to record audio when capturing video. Not used during photo capture.
- **Photo Library** — only if you turn on "Save to Photos." When enabled, the developed photo or video is saved to your system Photos library. LumBo Cam does not read your existing photos or videos unless you import one through the in-app picker.
- **Location, Contacts** — not used.

## What is stored on your device

- Photos and videos you capture or import, and the developed media produced by the engine, in LumBo Cam's app-private folder.
- Film recipes you create or favorite, and the recipe snapshots locked to each roll.
- App preferences (such as the RAW Capture toggle and the Save to Photos toggle).

This data stays on your device. You can delete all of it at any time by removing the app.

## TestFlight builds

If you are using LumBo Cam through TestFlight, Apple collects diagnostic and usage information per Apple's own TestFlight beta tester data practices. LumBo Cam itself does not receive this information.

## Children

LumBo Cam is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change. Material changes will be noted in the app's release notes.
