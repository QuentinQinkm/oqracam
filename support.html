Email support

Found a bug or have a feature request? Email fantasyqkm@gmail.com with your device model, your iOS version, and a screenshot if you can.

No account, no servers — your photos, videos, and films stay on your device. See the Privacy Policy for the details.

What you need

An iPhone running iOS 18 or later. The film look works on every iPhone — you don't need a Pro. The Camera Control button is an iPhone 16 feature.

What it shoots

You just point and shoot — Oqra automatically captures the best quality your iPhone supports: ProRAW on Pro models, high-quality HEIF on the rest. It shoots Live Photos and video up to 30 seconds too, and every frame is developed with the film you've loaded.

Resolution

Full-resolution photos, up to 48 MP on supported iPhones.

How it works

Your iPhone captures the light; Oqra develops it on-device through a film engine modeled on real photochemistry — spectral exposure, characteristic curves, dye, grain, and halation — and shows you the developed result live in the viewfinder, before you even press the shutter. The look is computed from the light, never laid on top, and nothing ever leaves your phone.
